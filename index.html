About

Educator,
Researcher & Scholar

Dr. Lijun "Lizzie" Chen is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Information Systems at Appalachian State University's Walker College of Business, located in Boone, North Carolina.

Her work bridges the fields of information systems, data analytics, and business technology, contributing both to academic scholarship and practical applications in the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Dr. Chen is committed to fostering an inclusive and intellectually stimulating learning environment where students develop critical thinking and technical skills to thrive in the modern workforce.
